Where to Eat in Osaka

Osaka is Japan's kitchen! Here's a quick guide to the best local dishes and where to find them.

  • Takoyaki: Octopus-filled batter balls, best tried at street stalls in Dotonbori.
  • Okonomiyaki: A savory pancake with cabbage, meat, and topped with special sauces.
  • Kushikatsu: Deep-fried skewers of meat and veggies, perfect from Shinsekai eateries.
  • Ramen & Udon: Available throughout the city in cozy local shops.
